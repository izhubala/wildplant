OVERVIEW

FARO®‘s Focus Laser Scanner series captures the as-built digital replica of a physical building or environment. the Photorealistic ready to use 3D images generated from the faro 3D scanner are highly detailed and textured and accurately represent the live terrestrial objects. Faro 3D scanner has an intuitive touch screen for better 3D viewing of live data capturing. Its built-in eight mega-pixel HDR camera captures natural colour overlay to the 3D laser scan data in extreme lighting conditions. In addition to this, it is lightweight, easy to carry, and small in size enables a per day 3D scanning of 8 plus hours with its battery capacity of 4.5 hours of scanning each time. The backup battery will add another 4.5 hours of non-stop 3D scanning.

KEY FEATURES

High-Level of Accuracy and Range (up to 350m)

Get exceptional accuracy with a combination of the most advanced laser sensor technologies.

Great Flexibility

3D scanning surveyor can Hand carry faro focus 3d scanner to on-site 3d scanning locations such as factories indoors and outdoors, also can 3d scan in direct sunlight or complete darkness.

Strong and Rugged Design

Faro focus got Ingress Protection (IP) and Class 54 environmental ratings specifically designed to be extensively used reliably in harsh environmental conditions, such as solid vibration areas, extreme temperatures, and wet or dusty environments.

High Dynamic Range (HDR) Photo Overlay to 3D scan

There can be challenging lighting situations, but Faro's focus ensures texture and color while you can. You can view 3D points lifelike visualization and high definition color detail superimposed to the 3D point cloud.

Various Sensors

Faro Focus 3D scanner is inbuilt with multiple sensors, including GPS, compass, height, and dual-axis compensator.

Touch screen and Scanner Control

Operate the scanner and view the live scanning with a touch screen and remotely control it with any mobile device via a WLAN connection

Retake Photos Option

Retake the photos on a selection basis, without the need to redo entire scans.

Multiple 3D Scan Group

At any point during the 3D scanning or after finishing the scan, if you need to 3D scan again a specific area with a high-resolution scan, you do it without redoing the entire scan.

Digital Hashing

The automatic digital hashing feature allows encrypted security to all raw 3D scans by hashing data at the time of 3D scanning.

On-site Compensation

The On-Site Compensation process is a procedure that allows users to test and improve the angular accuracy of the scanner using SCENE software before starting a scan on-site

On-site Registration option

Whether it can be a single scan or multiple scans, you can wirelessly transfer the scans from the scanner to the computer while ensuring auto-registration of the scan in real-time.

Integrated Accessory Bay

The interface lets you connect the faro focus scanner into various accessories designed for automation and customization.

Laser Status Indicator

Use laser status indicator while charging, scanning or while performing any major operation , to ensure on-site safety with laser status indicator.
