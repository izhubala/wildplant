3D laser Scanning Technology - Long-Range

The Laser Scanner releases a beam of infrared laser light onto a rotating mirror that effectively reflects the surrounding object or area with light. A scanner head rotates in long-range laser scanning technology and projects the laser across the environment around it scans. In the laser path the object reflect the beam back to the scanner , providing the digital replica that's transformed in to 3D Data, This scanner rotates vertical angle and horizontal angel making it possible to capture the complete 360 degree photorealistic 3D mage.
