FAQ

We use lidar and drones 3d scanning equipment to 3d scan the forest area.

By 3d scanning any forest, you can create a 3d walkthrough your forest in AR & VR , manage your forest assets such as big trees and streams, 3d planning for your resort construction or agriculture development

3d laser scanning applications in forests and trees include digital surveying, Digital volume calculation of caves and mines, and measurements of tree parameters.

Estimation of forest scanning is based on the area and application. We charge 40 to 50k INR or scan a forest.

We can 3D scan one acre of forest land with trees and plants per day, considering 8 hours of nonstop scanning time.
