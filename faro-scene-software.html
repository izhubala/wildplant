Forensic scene digital capture

The easiest technique to reliably preserve evidence is to use a laser scanner to digitally capture a crime, crash, or fire scene, but this creates a lot of data. SCENE software is the greatest tool for processing registration and managing scan data, resulting in a visually stunning point cloud of the scene for crime scene investigation, fire and arson investigation, crash reconstruction, and other applications.

Improved Safety Planning and Failure Investigation

provides a more detailed picture of the situation and identify possible pre-incident hazards, potentially saving lives and preventing future injuries. SCENE is a helpful tool for safety planning and failure investigation because of its virtual 3D on-site viewing capabilities.
